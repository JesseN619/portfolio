About Me

My name is Jesse Nauman. I was born and raised in the suburbs of Pittsburgh, PA. Currently, I live in Chicagoland with my wife and our dog, Phoebe.

I have a bachelor's and a master's degree in exercise science from the University of Pittsburgh. After several years of working in weight management, health screening, and even as a clinician in a doctor's office, I decided that I needed a career change.

In my free time, I began learning the basics of Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoyed coding, although, intially, I wasn't sure I could really turn it into a career without a CS degree. After a friend completed a bootcamp and got hired, I decided to quit my job and put everything I have into creating a new career.

Working as a clinician revealed many inefficiencies and problems with the healthcare industry. My ultimate goal is to use my past experiences with new tech skills to work in the healthcare software industry and improve the quality of work-life for clinicians and the quality of care for patients.
